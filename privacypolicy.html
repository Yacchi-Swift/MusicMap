Music Map – Privacy Policy

Last Updated:November 23, 2025

Music Map values your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

1. Data Collected

Music Map collects information only on your device when you press the "Log" button:

  • Song Information: title, artist, date, and time
  • Location: where you pressed the Log button

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email, or device ID.

2. How Data is Used

All collected data is stored locally on your device. It is used only to:

  • View your listening history
  • Explore logs on the map
  • Replay songs to relive your memories

Data is not sent to any servers or third parties.

3. Permissions

  • Location: used only when logging a song
  • Music Access (optional): used only to read currently playing song

Permissions are optional. Some features may be limited if denied.

4. Third-Party Services

Music Map does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party tracking services. All data remains on your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

Music Map does not collect personal information from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Your Rights

  • Deleting the app removes all data from your device
  • Permissions can be revoked at any time in your device settings

Because data never leaves your device, you have full control over your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
